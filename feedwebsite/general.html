General

Step A: Mix the following in a bin

  • 0.75lb shrimp choped into 1/4 inch pices (include shells and tails)
  • 1.5lb smelt chopped into 1/4 inch pices
  • 0.5lb gel chop into 1/4pices
  • 0.5llb capelin chop into 1/4 inch pices
  • 0.35lb squid
    • Chop tentacles off of head (use for squirt bottles)
    • Chop bodies into 1/4 inch pices
  • 0.75 medium carnivore pellets

Step B:

  • Add 1/2 cup of Vitafish liquid and mix well
  • Divide food evenly between two general food buckets
