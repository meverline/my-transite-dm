Squirt Bottles

Mix the following items and divide among 4-6 squirt bottles (as needed)

  • 0.10lb squid tentacles choped finely
  • 0.5lb shrimp remove shell and tails and chop finely
  • 1.0lb gel chop into pea-sized pices
  • 0.7llb small krill
  • 0.25lb small carnivore pellets
  • 5 sheets of Nori - shred by hand

  • Fill two flake bottles up to the red line with crushed flak
